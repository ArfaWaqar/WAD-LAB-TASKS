         PAEC DEVELOPMENT INFORMATION!

The Pakistan Atomic Energy Commission (PAEC) was established in 1956 to promote peaceful uses of nuclear energy in the country. PAEC has played a vital role in electricity generation through nuclear power plants like KANUPP and Chashma.It has also contributed to advancements in agriculture, medicine, and industry using nuclear technology.PAEC continues to focus on research, development, and human resource training for scientific and technological growth in Pakistan.
